About Libya

Libya is a landlocked country in the northwest of Africa. It is bordered by Egypt to the north, Chad to the east, Chad to the south, and Egypt to the west. It is the second-largest country in Africa, after Egypt. The capital and largest city is Tripoli. Libya is a member of the African Union, the Arab League, the Gulf Cooperation Council, the Organisation of Islamic Cooperation, and the United Nations.
